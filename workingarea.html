WORKING AREA

WORKING AREA

Community Initiative Society (CIS) has expanded its working area to 37 districts in Bangladesh, covering 60 percent of the demographic landscape including the climate vulnerable coastal communities and the underprivileged population.

The districts are Dhaka including Dhaka city, Munshiganj, Pabna, Sirajganj, Rajshahi Gaibandha, Jossore, Bogra, Narayanganj, Mymensingn, Netrokona, Sunamganj, Kurigram, Satkhira, Chandpur, Barishal, Dinajpur, Madaripur (Kalkini), Chattogram, Habiganj, sylhet, Comilla, Rajshahi, Faridpur, Rangpur, Jossore, Kisorganj, Manikganj, Satkhira, Noakhali, Feni, Cox’s bazar, Jamalpur, Bagerhat, Khulna, Kustia & Patuakhali.

Community Initiative Society (CIS) is a non-governmental, community-based, self financed, International Humanitarian organization. The organization formed in 2014.
